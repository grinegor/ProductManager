# Competitor Pattern Notes

## Common Winning Patterns
- Personalized quiz for perceived relevance.
- Intro discount paired with transparent renewal terms.
- In-app streaks and simple adherence progress visuals.

## Common Failure Patterns
- Overpromising weight outcomes in ad creative.
- Hidden subscription terms causing high month-1 churn.
- Weak post-purchase onboarding and no habit-building loop.

## PM Takeaway
Sustainable growth usually comes from balanced funnel conversion + month-2 retention, not top-funnel scale alone.
