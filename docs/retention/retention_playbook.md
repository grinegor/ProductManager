# Retention Playbook for Health Subscriptions

## Critical Moments
- Day 0-3: expectation setting and setup completion.
- Day 7: first visible progress checkpoint.
- Day 21-28: habit durability and first renewal anxiety.

## Churn Levers
- Improve onboarding completion and first-value speed.
- Create plan adherence nudges (non-medical, behavior oriented).
- Trigger save offers at cancellation intent with plan pause and dosage reminders.
- Use segmented win-back campaigns by cancellation reason.

## Unit Economics Framing
- LTV should be modeled by cohort, not blended averages only.
- Payback target often < 3 months for paid acquisition stability.
- Trial conversion and month-2 retention are leading indicators for sustainable CAC.
