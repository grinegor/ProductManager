# Health Ad Compliance Guardrails

## Risky Claims to Avoid
- "Lose 20 pounds in 2 weeks guaranteed"
- "Clinically proven for everyone"
- "Permanent results without effort"

## Safer Messaging
- Focus on support, routine, and personalized plans.
- Use qualified language: "may support", "designed to help".
- Avoid implying diagnosis/treatment unless clearly regulated and allowed.

## Platform Constraints (Practical)
- Meta and Google often restrict sensational body-image language.
- Avoid before/after framing that implies guaranteed body transformation.
- Keep ad-to-landing consistency; policy-safe ad copy must match destination claims.
